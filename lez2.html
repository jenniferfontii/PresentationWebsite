I have joined the travel and culture society in september and I have been to 4 meetings.

Because of covid I moved to Nottingham without ever being here before, I found myself quite loneley expecially the first weeks, so I decided to join a society. After searching for a long time I found the perfect fit for me, I have always loved to travel and meet new people from other countries. I went to the first meeting hoping to find friends and learn more about this city and I did. This society helped me a lot to feel less lonely and I met great people that helped me whenever I wasn't in my best mood.
